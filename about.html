About Me


Dylan Speiser

Dylan has been making movies for almost as long as he's been alive. Honing his skills since the day he grasped a camera, Dylan excels at cinematography, editing, and live production. Dylan also enjoys acting in front of the camera, as well as onstage. He pours his soul into storytelling, using the most cutting-edge technology he can find. Of course, none of it would be possible without the unwavering support of Dylan's friends and family, who, for some reason, participate in his shenanigans.
